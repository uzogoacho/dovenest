The Legacy

A Sanctuary of Private Luxury

Located in the heart of St. Cruz, Goa, Dove Nest was born from a vision to create a space where intimacy meets celebration. We aren't just a resort; we are a private canvas for your most cherished memories.

Our Philosophy

We believe luxury is found in privacy. Our venue is exclusively yours for the duration of your stay, ensuring a personalized atmosphere that no public resort can match.

Tropical Design

Inspired by modern Goan architecture, Dove Nest blends sleek charcoal aesthetics with lush tropical greens and warm ambient lighting.

Dove Caterers

Culinary excellence is at our core. Our in-house catering team specializes in authentic Indian hospitality and premium global cuisines.
